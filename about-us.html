About the African Biodiversity Conference

The African Biodiversity Conference brings together leading scientists, conservationists, and policymakers to discuss solutions for sustainable ecosystems across the continent.

Our goal is to promote knowledge sharing, encourage environmental stewardship, and empower communities to take action for biodiversity conservation.
