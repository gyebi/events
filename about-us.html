About the African Biodiversity Conference

The African Biodiversity Conference brings together leading scientists, conservationists, and policymakers to discuss solutions for sustainable ecosystems across the continent.

Our goal is to promote knowledge sharing, encourage environmental stewardship, and empower communities to take action for biodiversity conservation.

Our Mission

To foster collaboration and innovation in biodiversity conservation through research, education, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A thriving Africa where biodiversity is preserved, ecosystems are resilient, and communities coexist harmoniously with nature.

Our Partners

We collaborate with a diverse range of partners including universities, NGOs, government agencies, and international organizations to amplify our impact and reach.
