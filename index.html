The Venue

Ghana is wildlife protection and habitat/resource conservation for the sustainable benefits of present and future generations. The Division therefore educates the public and fringe communities on the wise use of resources, and managing the protected areas for eco-tourism is one of such uses. Nature enthusiasts, students, volunteers and researchers have found out how much Ghana offers by way of wildlife and wildlife habitats some of which offer good opportunities for adventure.
